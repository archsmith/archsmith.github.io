Embarcadero 10

  • Miami, Fl
  • Miami River Coffee Center
  • Professor Edgar Sarli
  • Spring 2015
  • Partner: Rhys Gilbertson
  • Best of Class, Finalist

Embarcadero 10 is located along the Miami River, just walking distance from Downtown Miami. The program includes a coffee aspect including a cafe, storage/shipping of raw coffee, and roasting raw coffee, a local goods market, retail spaces, office spaces, and a community hall. In order to address the tight constraints of a narrow and awkwardly shaped site, an attempt was made to contain the program in an uncompromisingly lengthy form. A light long bar building was conceived atop a heavy concrete plinth that conforms to the site in contrast with the building above. This formed a strong duality that led the materiality of the project to develop with dark walnut wood louvers enveloping the building contrasting with the bright and smooth concrete finish below.
